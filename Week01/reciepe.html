Gulab Jamun Recipe

Ingredients Instructions
  • 1 cup of milk powder
  • 1/4 cup of all-purpose flour
  • 1/4 cup of ghee (clarified butter)
  • 1/4 cup of milk
  • 1/4 tsp of baking soda
  • A pinch of cardamom powder
  • Oil for deep frying
  1. In a mixing bowl, combine milk powder, all-purpose flour, ghee, milk, baking soda, and cardamom powder.
  2. Knead the mixture into a soft dough.
  3. Divide the dough into small portions and shape them into smooth balls.
  4. Heat oil in a pan over medium heat.
  5. Fry the dough balls until they turn golden brown.
  6. Remove the fried balls and drain excess oil on a paper towel.
  7. Prepare sugar syrup by dissolving 1 cup of sugar in 1 cup of water over medium heat.
  8. Boil the syrup until it reaches a one-string consistency.
  9. Drop the fried dough balls into the hot sugar syrup and let them soak for a few hours.
  10. Gulab Jamuns are ready to serve once they absorb the syrup and become soft.
